Did you know it ?

The French language is widely used throughout the world and in the worldwide economy by more than 200 million of French-speaking professionals. French is the official language of diplomacy and business in the 84 states and governments who are members of the International Organisation of La Francophonie, which stand for 20% of the international trade. Learning French is undoubtfully a great asset for your professional career.
