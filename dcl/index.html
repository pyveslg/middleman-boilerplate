The “Diplôme de Compétence en Langue” (DCL)

The ”diplôme de compétence en langue” (DCL) is a professional state certification specifically designed for adults from the professional world by the French Ministry of National Education.

The DCL certifies the gained skills in the professional and daily language communication commonly used in all the economic activities. Its uniqueness lies on its candidates' language level assessment process. The candidates are put in a work position close to reality.

To know more about DCL, you may access to the official website (in French)

A certificate specially designed for adult professionals

The examination is based on both written and spoken communication through a simulation close to work reality

Unlimited validity certificate with one of the levels from the Common European Framework of Reference for Languages (CECRL)
